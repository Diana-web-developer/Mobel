About Møbel.

In 1984 our founder started working for the family construction business helping homeowners design their new areas of their home. With decades of experience and talents she opened up her own design and staging business that became the largest design and staging company in the Inland Empire. With thousands of satisfied customers over the years the company has blossomed into Møbel. now giving homeowners throughout the nation the tips on how to make their homes a more comfortable place to live.

Our Green Project
We have initiated The Green Project to include environmentally friendly products for you to browse throughout your HouseTie experience and include in your next home projects.

Our Quality Brands
We do the research for you and find the best quality products for you and your home. From the kitchen to the bathroom and every room in the house and the outside from the lighting to the landscaping we've got it covered.

Shipping

Shipping Policies
Møbel. understands that getting your items to you in a timely manner is important to you, so we process your order swiftly. You can expect to receive most items within 4-5 business days. Please note that because we make every effort to ship your product as quickly as possible, once your item(s) have shipped we are unable to make any changes to your order. If there are additional items that you want to have shipped you can start a separate order.

About Free Shipping
Items $50 and over Ship FREE, via standard ground service, to locations within the contiguous United States. Additional charges apply for shipments to Alaska and Hawaii. Most orders ship via UPS Ground, Fed Ex, USPS 1st class or USPS Priority.

Flat-Rate Shipping
Orders under $50 ship for just $9.95, via standard ground service, to locations within the contiguous United States.

Next-Day Air
Next-business-day delivery by 4:30 p.m. local time to most U.S. addresses; by 8p.m. to rural areas. No weekend delivery.

Ground Shipping
Up to 5 Business Days (does not include processing time or weekends). Keep in mind that large appliances, larger vanities and oversized furniture may take several extra days. Your package may be delivered on a Saturday if shipped via FedEx Home or USPS. Ground shipping is not available to Alaska or Hawaii or for P.O. Boxes or military APO/FPO addresses at this time.

Expedited shipping
Expedited shipping is available on certain small items at an additional charge and is non-refundable on returns. Expedited shipping on in-stock orders placed by 5pm ET Monday through Friday will ship the same day and will arrive on a business day, according to the shipment type chosen below.

3-Day Service
3 Business Days (does not include processing time). No weekend delivery. 2-Day Air Service 2 Business Days (does not include processing time). No weekend delivery.

Shipping Restrictions
At this time, we are currently do not ship to P.O. Boxes and Military APO/FPO addresses.
